Berta route and send data - Werkstatt

Made with Beta7 ♥ data.

Last update: 20/10/22


Each route has two plots differing only in style - but they show the same data: sender's height, grade, and #attemps (hover for #attemps in heat maps)

The third plot compares the distribution of senders' grade, height, and #attemps between the climbers of the route (pink) and all climbers of all routes of the routes grade (purple). Although p values (mann whitney u test) are reported (for p<0.05), please don't trust them as the n size is usually very small.
